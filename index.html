Interesting facts about chameleon :-

  • Chameleon are the reptiles that are part of iguana suborder
  • Changing skin colours is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have prehensile tails that they can use to wrap around tree branch

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are stout-bodied birds with short necks, and short slender bills that in some species feature fleshy ceres
  • Pigeon is a French word that derives from the Latin pipio, for a "peeping" chick
  • Pigeons primarily feed on seeds, fruits, and plants
